Frequently Asked Questions
What is Netflix? +

Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more on thousands of internet-connected devices. You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want without a single commercial – all for one low monthly price.

How much does Netflix cost? +

Netflix offers different plans starting from around $6.99 to $22.99 per month as of 2025, depending on the region and plan features (Basic, Standard, Premium). Prices may vary by country and include options for HD and Ultra HD streaming, as well as the number of simultaneous streams.

Where can I watch? +

You can watch Netflix on smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, laptops, gaming consoles (like PlayStation and Xbox), streaming media players (like Chromecast and Apple TV), and more. Download the Netflix app or visit netflix.com to get started.

How do I cancel? +

You can cancel your Netflix subscription at any time. To cancel, log in to your account on the Netflix website, go to your Account page, and follow the instructions to cancel your membership. There are no cancellation fees, and you can continue to watch until the end of your billing period.

What can I watch on Netflix? +

Netflix offers a vast library of content including movies, TV shows, documentaries, stand-up comedy, and kids' programming. You can find popular titles, Netflix Originals, and content across various genres and for all ages.

Is Netflix good for kids? +

Yes, Netflix is great for kids! It offers a wide range of family-friendly content, including cartoons, educational shows, and movies. You can create kid profiles with parental controls, PIN protection, and a safe viewing experience tailored for children.
